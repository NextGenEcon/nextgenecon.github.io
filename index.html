👋 About the Founder

Hi, I’m Yuvraj Singh — a student passionate about economics, innovation, and global impact. I created NextGenEcon to unite like-minded students from across the world to explore, build, and lead in the field of economics.

🌟 Mission & Vision

Mission: To build a global platform that enables students to explore economics through competitions, community, and real-world projects.

Vision: To empower the next generation of economic leaders by providing resources, mentorship (in progress), and global collaboration.

🚀 What We Offer

  • 🌐 Global student community
  • 📚 Weekly economics challenges
  • 🎓 Aspiring mentorship and speaker sessions
  • 🛠️ Economics-based projects and case studies
  • 💼 Resume-boosting opportunities & collaboration
