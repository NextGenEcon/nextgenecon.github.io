About NGEL

The Next Gen Economics League (NGEL) is a youth-led national initiative integrating technology, research, and outreach to create measurable impact in financial literacy, economic empowerment, and scalable social innovation.

Technology

Builds financial tools, apps, and systems including LedgerPlus, ERP for vendors, and ML-powered economic solutions.

Research

Produces research reports, whitepapers, policy analysis, and evaluates impact across NGEL initiatives.

Projects

Oversees all NGEL impact ventures including LedgerPlus deployments, ERP rollouts, and new economic innovation projects.

Outreach

Runs workshops, collaborates with NGOs, grows community initiatives, and manages PR, partnerships, and volunteer operations.
