NextGenEcon

The Premier Youth League for the Future Leaders of Economics, Innovation, and Global Change.

🌍 The Mission

At NextGenEcon, we envision a world shaped by visionary youth who understand economics as a force for innovation and equity. Our mission is to unite elite high school students globally who are passionate about economic research, leadership, and social transformation.

🚀 Why Join?

This isn’t just a club—it’s a movement. As a member of NextGenEcon, you'll gain:

  • 🌐 Mentorship from economists, Ivy League alumni, and industry leaders
  • 🤝 Collaborations with like-minded peers on advanced research projects
  • 📚 Access to curated resources and real-world case studies
  • 🏆 Invitations to exclusive seminars, simulations, and challenges
  • 📣 A global platform to publish your economic research and blogs

🎓 What Makes Us Elite?

We carefully select members based on their curiosity, academic discipline, leadership spirit, and passion for change. Our league empowers students who want to explore cutting-edge topics like behavioral economics, climate finance, development economics, and AI in finance.

📈 Your Growth Journey

At NextGenEcon, you don't just participate—you lead. With each month, members ascend through the league’s ranks by:

  • 🧠 Leading economic debates & policy simulations
  • 📝 Publishing economic insights and research
  • 👨‍🏫 Teaching others through our Junior Fellowship Program
  • 🏛 Representing the league at international competitions
