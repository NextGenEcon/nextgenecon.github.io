About NGEL

The Next Gen Economics League (NGEL) is a youth-led national initiative integrating technology, research, and outreach to create measurable impact in financial literacy, economic empowerment, and scalable social innovation.

Technology Division

Our Technology Division builds all NGEL software, tools, and systems.

  • LedgerPlus – financial literacy mobile app
  • ERP System for Indian street vendors
  • Automation tools for outreach & reporting
  • Future ML-based economic modeling tools

Research Division

The Research Division analyzes economic issues and evaluates NGEL's impact.

  • Whitepapers on financial literacy effectiveness
  • Economic research & policy analysis
  • Impact reports on workshops & deployments
  • Collaboration with professors & NGOs

Flagship Projects

NGEL oversees high-impact economic empowerment projects.

  • LedgerPlus: app used in financial literacy workshops
  • ERP Vendor System: supporting small vendors in India
  • Future student-led economic-tech initiatives

Outreach & Operations

Leading national workshops, partnerships, and on-ground impact.

  • Financial literacy workshops across UAE & India
  • Partnerships with NGOs, schools & labor camps
  • Managing volunteer teams & community engagement
  • PR, social media & impact documentation
